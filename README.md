# EarthVerse AI 🌍

Don’t just calculate your carbon footprint. Experience the future consequences of your choices.

## Overview

EarthVerse AI is a futuristic AI-powered climate intelligence platform that transforms everyday lifestyle decisions into future environmental simulations, climate legacy scores, and sustainability insights.

Unlike traditional carbon calculators, EarthVerse AI creates an emotional connection between human behavior and the future of the planet through AI storytelling, behavioral analysis, and predictive climate intelligence.

---

## Problem Statement

Most carbon footprint applications only provide numbers and charts.

Users often fail to understand the long-term environmental impact of their daily actions.

EarthVerse AI solves this by converting carbon behavior into interactive future simulations, climate legacy analysis, and AI-generated personalized environmental narratives.

---

## Core Features

### Climate Legacy Score
Analyzes long-term environmental impact on future generations.

### AI Future Letter
Generates personalized letters from the year 2050 based on user habits.

### Parallel Timeline Simulation
Creates multiple future Earth scenarios based on different environmental choices.

### Climate Ripple Engine
Shows how small actions create long-term chain reactions.

### AI Climate Mentor Hub
Provides advice from:
- Earth AI
- Scientist AI
- Future Citizen AI

### Climate Passport
Gamified sustainability profile with levels, badges, and impact points.

### Humanity Impact Simulator
Answers:
"What if everyone behaved like you?"

### EcoDNA Personality Analysis
Assigns environmental behavior personality types.

### Earth Court AI
Simulates a climate courtroom for environmental judgment.

### Future Newspaper Generator
Creates AI-generated climate news from the future.
